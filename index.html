WHO ARE WE?

We at Shree Fabrics believe in manufacturing relationships and distributing quality. We manufacture and export a wide range of customisable products in line with our customers needs. We are your source for providing unmatched quality of disposable products at a low cost for all industries.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We are engaged in manufacturing, supplying, importing and exporting a comprehensive assortment of products for Spas, Salons, Hotels, Restaurants, Hospitals and Catering since 2007. Our products are manufactured using quality-tested raw materials that are procured from authentic and certified vendor bases. We have an autonomous infrastructure facility, equipped with the required instruments and machines to put together a vast range of products for all your needs.

QUALITY POLICY

The staff and management are committed to delight our customers with outstanding quality of our products and services. Our team of experienced quality auditors is responsible for maintaining the quality of our products as per the regulatory standards.
